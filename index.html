10 · QUESTIONS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

We believe in complete transparency. If you have a question not listed here, feel free to contact us direct.

Call Us (780) 720-8233

How long does a typical project take?

Most interior rooms are completed in 1-2 days, while full residential home painting projects typically take 3-5 days depending on the scope and weather conditions.

What brand of paint do you use?

We primarily use premium Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams products. These brands offer superior durability, color retention, and a wide range of high-end finishes.

Are you fully insured and licensed?

Yes, we carry $5,000,000 in General Liability insurance and all our professional painters are covered under WCB Alberta and WorkSafeBC for your total peace of mind.

Do you offer color consultations?

Absolutely. We help you choose the perfect palette that complements your lighting, furniture, and style, ensuring the final result is exactly as you envisioned.
